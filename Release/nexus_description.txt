[line]

[heading]How It Works[/heading]

[list=1]
[*]Open the [b]Hitman[/b] app on your phone and pay the [b]$5,000[/b] registration fee
[*]Press [b]REQUEST CONTRACT[/b] or wait for an automatic SMS offer
[*]Read the offer — target name, contract type, difficulty, and reward
[*]Tap [b]Accept[/b] or [b]Decline[/b] via SMS reply
[*]Use the [b]Phone Signal Tracker[/b] to hunt down your target
[*]Complete the job and collect your payout via bank transfer
[/list]

[line]

[heading]Two Contract Types[/heading]

[list]
[*][color=#E64050][b]Eliminate[/b][/color] — Kill the target. Clean and simple.
[*][color=#E6A040][b]Knock Out[/b][/color] — Incapacitate the target without killing them. Kill a knockout target and the contract fails.
[/list]

[heading]5 Difficulty Levels[/heading]

Targets don't know you're coming. They go about their day until you make your move. Once you attack, they fight back — and how they fight depends on the difficulty:

[list]
[*][color=#00C878][b]Very Easy[/b][/color] ($500–$1,500) — Unarmed, barely fights back
[*][color=#00C878][b]Easy[/b][/color] ($1,500–$3,000) — Grabs a broken bottle
[*][color=#E6C040][b]Medium[/b][/color] ($3,000–$5,500) — Pulls a knife, 1.5x health
[*][color=#E68040][b]Hard[/b][/color] ($5,500–$9,000) — Draws a pistol, 2x health
[*][color=#E64050][b]Very Hard[/b][/color] ($9,000–$15,000) — Pulls a shotgun, 3x health
[/list]

Targets only arm themselves [b]after you attack[/b]. Before that, they look and act completely normal.

[line]

[heading]Phone Signal Tracker[/heading]

After accepting a contract, the Hitman app shows a live [b]Phone Signal Tracker[/b] for your target:

[list]
[*][b]Live distance[/b] — Shows how far the target is from you in real time (color-coded: [color=#00C878]green[/color] = close, [color=#E6C040]yellow[/color] = medium, [color=#E6A040]orange[/color] = far)
[*][b]Region intel[/b] — Displays the target's current region (Docks, Downtown, Northtown, Suburbia, Uptown, Westville)
[*][b]SMS updates[/b] — Your client also sends you text messages when the target moves to a different region
[/list]

Intel accuracy depends on difficulty:

[list]
[*][color=#00C878][b]Very Easy / Easy[/b][/color] — Full tracking: live distance + exact region intel
[*][color=#E6C040][b]Medium[/b][/color] — Live distance + mostly accurate region, occasionally vague
[*][color=#E68040][b]Hard[/b][/color] — Live distance + always vague region descriptions
[*][color=#E64050][b]Very Hard[/b][/color] — One initial update only. After that, you're on your own.
[/list]

[line]

[heading]Reputation System (Level 1–10)[/heading]

Every completed contract earns reputation. As your rep grows, harder contracts unlock:

[list]
[*][b]Level 1–2[/b] — Very Easy and Easy contracts only
[*][b]Level 3–4[/b] — Medium contracts unlocked
[*][b]Level 5–7[/b] — Hard contracts unlocked
[*][b]Level 8–10[/b] — Very Hard contracts unlocked
[/list]

Your reputation level, progress bar, completed contracts, and total earnings are all displayed in the phone app.

[heading]Smart Target Selection[/heading]

The mod automatically excludes NPCs that shouldn't be targets:

[list]
[*][b]Dealers[/b] — All dealer NPCs (yours and others') are excluded
[*][b]Employees[/b] — Your botanists, chemists, cleaners, and packagers
[*][b]Suppliers[/b] — All supplier NPCs
[*][b]Goons[/b] — Cartel goons and bodyguards
[*][b]Officers[/b] — Police, guards, and enforcers
[*][b]Story NPCs[/b] — Quest-essential and vendor characters
[/list]

Only regular citizens can become targets — no one essential to your business or the game's systems.

[heading]Cooldown[/heading]

After completing or failing a contract, there's a [b]cooldown[/b] before you can take on the next one. The remaining time is displayed in the app.
