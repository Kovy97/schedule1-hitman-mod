T.H.M - The Hitman Mod - Become a Hitman
========================================
Version 3.0.0
Author: Kowyx

REQUIREMENTS
------------
- MelonLoader (latest version)
- S1API (Il2Cpp MelonLoader) v2.8.8+

INSTALLATION
------------
1. Install MelonLoader and S1API if you haven't already.
2. Copy HitmanMod.dll into your "Schedule I/Mods/" folder.
3. Launch the game.

HOW TO PLAY
-----------
1. Open your phone and find the "Hitman" app.
2. Register as a hitman for $5,000 (paid via bank transfer).
3. Press "REQUEST CONTRACT" or wait for automatic SMS offers.
4. Accept or decline contracts via SMS.
5. Use location intel to find your target.
6. Eliminate or knock out the target to get paid.
7. Level up your reputation to unlock harder contracts.

UNINSTALL
---------
Delete HitmanMod.dll from the Mods folder.
